The Hunt for the Red Jurassic

The Movie-Mashup No One Knew They Wanted


Starring an all-star duo:


Sean Connery as "Marko Ramius"

Velociraptor as "Velociraptor"

Tyrannosaurus Rex as "Rex"

Alec Baldwin as "Jack Ryan"



Summoned to a remote island above the Arctic Circle, Soviet submarine Captain Marko Ramius discvoers that an eccentric admiral has created an undetectable submarine powered by the icthyosaur drive. But Captain Ramius intends to defect. Only two CIA agents, one a raptor and his partner a man named Jack Ryan, knows this. It's a race against time, the forces of both militaries, and the sea itself. Join Sean Connery as he plays a dangerous hiding game aboard the beast known as "The Red Jurassic"
