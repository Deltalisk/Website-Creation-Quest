The Hunt for the Red Jurassic

The Movie-Mashup No One Knew They Wanted


Starring an all-star duo:


Velociraptor as "Velociraptor"

Sean Connery as "Marko Ramius"



Set before the events of "The Hunt for Red October", Submarine Captain Marko Ramius was at the height of his military career. But not only did the Soviets have advanced submersibles. They had mastered the genetic code of a beast long thought extinct. After rescuing one of the Soviet's "experiments", join Sean Connery as he plays a dangerous hiding game with the beast known as "Red Jurassic"
